About us

Heartwood Rock & Electro festival is located at Tsugaiken Kogen Ski Resort in Nagano. High up in the mountains, away from the fuzz of daily life Heartwood festival is the perfect place to take time off, listen to good music and making new friend!

Heartwood festival is a "green festival" and sustainability is very important to us. Our motto is "Love music, love green" and our goal is to create a enviromental friendly festival.

Energy saving

We have solar panels installed on the stageroofs. We’re committed to offsetting the power generated at the Heartwood festival.

No plastic bottles

We don't sell any drinks in plastic bottles, all of our cups, plates, and utensils are 100% compostable at Heartwood festival. If you buy our official tumbler you get a discount on drinks and free access to the water station during the whole festival.

Recycle station

Help us recycle our garbage and collect points you can trade in for cool merchandise and food!

Organic food

We're doing our best to keep the food at Heartwood Fetival organic and healthy. Together with Canadian Farm and Organic Farm 88 we will be selling seasonal food, fruits & vegetables during the festival weekend.

Getting here

We are located in Tsugaiken Kogen ski resort close to the Hakuba Valley in Nagano. You can get here by bus from Nagano or Hakuba. We also offer travel packages from several cities that takes you directly to the festiaval.

Parking

We have to big parking areas located close to the camping and festival area. The parking is free for everyone with a ticket to the festival! A special parking for caravans are also available at a cheap price, which you can book along with your festival ticket.

Shuttlebus

We offer free shuttlebuses from the camping area into Hakuba valley every half hour. In Hakuba Valley you can find several shops and restuarant as well as hotels.

Train

We offer train + bus packages from several cities around Japan. The package includes Shinkansen to Nagano Station and a bus from Nagano Station all the way to the camping area. You can buy the travel package along with your ticket, for more information about which cities are included in the package click here.

Charity

Global warming is the result of excess greenhouse gases, created by burning fossil fuels and destroying tropical rainforests. Trees conserves energy, provides oxygen and saves water. Planting a tree will ensure forests and clean water persist for future generations which is why Heartwood plants a tree in needed areas around the world for every 10th ticket sold!
