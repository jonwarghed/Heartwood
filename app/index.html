About us

Heartwood Rock & Electro festival is located at Tsugaikekogen Ski Resort in Nagano. Far up in the mountains, away form the city and surrounded by beautiful nature. Heartwood festival is the perfect place to enjoy good music, meet new friends and just having a great time.

Our motto is "Love music, love green", sustainability and environmental consciousness is very important to us.

Energy saving

We’re committed to offsetting the power generated at the Heartwood festival. We have installed solar panels on the stageroofs.

No plastic bottles

We don't sell any drinks in plastic bottles and all of our cups, plates, and utensils are 100% compostable at Heartwood festival. We offer a refillable water program where you can buy our official tumbler and get free access to our water stations during the whole festival.

Recycle station

Help us keep the festival clean by recycling your trash! By sorting and bringing your trash to the recycle station you earn points you can trade in for cool merchandise or food!

Organic food

We try to keep our festival healthy and use as much organic ingredients as we can. Canadian Farm and Organic Farm 88 will be selling seasonal food, fruits & vegetables during the whole festival weekend.
